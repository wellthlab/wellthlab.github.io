Experiment in a Box: Account Deletion

Account Deletion Instructions for Experiment in a Box (XB)

In order to request your account is deleted, you have two options:

  1. If the app is still installed on your device, log in to the account you wish to delete. Navigate to settings, click 'Delete Account' then select 'OK'.
  2. Send an email to the Wellthlab Team at test@test.com and request account deletion.

In either case, a member of the Wellthlab Team will delete your account details from our records at the earliest possibility.
